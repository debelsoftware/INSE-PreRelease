Brand Guidelines:

These guidelines are a set of rules for the use of the Maker brand. These are to be used by our internal developers and any third parties wanting to use our brand. Failure to follow these guidelines when using our brand will lead us to terminate the use of our brand in your service.

The purpose of these rules is to ensure a consistent and seamless experience for anyone using our service and any related services using our brand.

Our Logo:

Rules:

  • The logo MUST be at least 10px clear of any other elements on the page
  • The logo MUST NOT be skewed, stretched or distorted
  • The logo MUST NOT be modified in any way. This includes but is not limited to adding effects, changing colours and changing fonts

Logo with Maker the seal
